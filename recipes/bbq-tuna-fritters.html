BBQ Tuna Fritters

Description

A fun way to make canned tuna taste less like canned tuna. These thin tuna patties are tasty enough to be eaten plain or in a pita pocket with lettuce and tomato.

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 ounce) can light tuna in water, drained
  • 1 egg
  • ⅔ cup quick-cooking oats
  • 3 tablespoons barbeque sauce
  • 3 tablespoons chopped green onion
  • ½ teaspoon hot pepper sauce, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon dried savory
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, stir together the tuna, egg and oats until blended.
  2. Mix in the barbeque sauce, green onion, hot pepper sauce, savory, and salt.
  3. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Spoon tablespoonfuls of the tuna mixture into the pan, and flatten slightly. Smaller patties hold together better.
  5. Cook until browned on each side, about 3 minutes per side.
