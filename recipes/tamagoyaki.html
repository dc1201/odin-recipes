Tamagoyaki Recipe

Description

Tamagoyaki is a type of Japanese omelette which is made by rolling together several layers of fried beaten eggs. It is often prepared in a rectangular omelette pan called a makiyakinabe or tamagoyakiki.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce, or to taste (Optional)
  • Â½ teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Mix water, soy sauce, and sugar together in a small bowl until sugar is dissolved. Add egg and beat until egg mixture is combined.
  2. Heat skillet over medium heat until a drop of water evaporates almost immediately.
  3. Pour egg mixture into pan. Cook until the bottom of the omelet has solidified, 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Flip and fold omelet into a square and transfer to a plate.
